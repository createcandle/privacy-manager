Internal logs

The internal logs can be automatically deleted every hour.


If you connect a PeriPage Bluetooth printer, then you can print your log data to paper at regular intervals. For example, each day at midnight you could print all the data that was collected during the day. After the print is complete, the log's data will be deleted from the system. So at the end of the day, the paper print will be the only copy of your data that remains.

This is an interesting option to have for very sensitive data. For example, you could print data about your health, and be sure that no digital copy remains.

Print rotation

You can rotate the image before it's printed. Using 270 degrees looks great, but will use more paper. If you set it to automatic, it will switch between no rotation and 270 degrees depending on how much data there is to print.

Be advised: if you have set the log to be printed at a rotation of 270 degrees, and if you have a large amount of collected data, then the print may be quite long.

Help

This addon showcases a numer of privacy innovations that were conceptualised within the Candle Smart Home project. It's goal is to explore how we may protect privacy better, and improve and safeguard people's trust in smart systems.

Beauty filters

People use filter on their social media posts to remove things like wrinkles in their skin. If other people are getting to see your data, you may wish to massage it to look a little more socially acceptible too. That's what this section is for: experimenting with making your data "look good". This is useful because in some situations we're seen smart homes being used to surveil, shame or control people, sometimes even without their knowing.

Data sculptor

Think of this as a precise scalpel to manipulate your data. You can remove, modify or even create data points. This is useful if, for example, you are planning a surprise birthday party and don't want your partner to figure out that you've been preparing.

Internal Logs

The control keeps internal logs of everything that happens in the system. Viewing these can give insight into behaviour, even if the other privacy protecting options are used. That's why you can delete the internal logs here.

Print

By connecting a little Bluetooth printer, you can make sure that your sensor data isn't even stored on the smart home controller anymore. The data is turned into a paper trail, giving a new level of control over your data by making the data tangible. With a paper printout you can be sure there is only one copy, and it's very easy to dispose of that copy.

If you were, for example, collecting data about health or sexual activity, then this option might be very valuable.

Debugging is enabled

This addon is currently set to save detailed information about its operation in the internal logs. This may impact your privacy, as this also means that insights into your behaviour might be stored in those logs.
